Scriptwriting

Screenwriting is the act of writing a story for the screen, a word that can refer to a variety of media. Although the words screenwriter and scriptwriter are often used interchangeably, the terms differ in meaning. A screenwriter writes a story specifically designed to be viewed on a screen.
